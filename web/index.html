RiverSafe

This project is made by Jonathan, Zak and Isaac. We are students at Fulford School who are passionate about the world and have the skills to help save it. Special thanks to Mr Dring, without whom this project would not have been possible.

RiverSafe is designed to test the pollution levels of water, to help combat big companies putting sewage into rivers, and to spread awareness of it. The dial bellow shows the current water pollution that is being picked up by the sensor attached to our RaspberryPi Pico. (Work In Progress: It's just random at the moment)
