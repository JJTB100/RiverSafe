RiverSafe

We are students at Fulford School who are passionate about the world, so we made RiverSafe.

RiverSafe is designed to test the pollution levels of water, to help combat big companies putting sewage into rivers, and to spread awareness of it.

This shows the current water pollution so you can see if it's safe to swim.

This shows how warm it is outside by the water so you can decide if you want to swim or not.

This is a useful table containing what the Pollution Level seen measured here actually means. As you can see, most tap water will be 100-400, but anything over that can be dangerous.
