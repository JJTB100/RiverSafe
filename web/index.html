RiverSafe

This project is made by Jonathan, who made it functional; Zak, who came up with the idea; Isaac, who made the media; and Sally, who made the logo. We are students at Fulford School who are passionate about the world and have the skills to help save it. Message from Mr Dring: Well done and thank you for all your hard work. Here's a video showing the project working so far

RiverSafe is designed to test the pollution levels of water, to help combat big companies putting sewage into rivers, and to spread awareness of it. The dial to the left shows the current water pollution that is being picked up by the sensor attached to our RaspberryPi Pico.
This is a useful table containing what the Pollution Level seen measured here actually means. As you can see, most tap water will be 100-400, but anythin over that can be dangerous.
